Research Interests

My research focuses on trustworthy multimodal AI for human–robot collaboration, using transformer-based models for context understanding, cognitive validation, ambiguity-aware interaction, and robot task planning in smart manufacturing.

Core areas: Multimodal Human-Robot Interaction, Human-Robot Collaboration, Transformer-based AI, Trustworthy AI, Cognitive Robotics, Robot Task Planning, and Smart Manufacturing.
